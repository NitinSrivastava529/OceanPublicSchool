OUR HISTORY

Ocean public school is a co- educational institution established in the year 2011 with a vision to impart integrated education with quality and value to students form all parts blending with the modern concepts in education to develop the total personality and to improve their self confidence and initiatives.

OPS prepares the children to face the challenges of living and working in the modern world.

OPS is running under the aegis of Jai Maa Sharda Shiksha Sewa Samiti and is Affiliated to U.P. State Government .
